Petals

Petals are used by the flower to defend itself. They are obtained by killing mobs. Petals come in 8 different rarities: common, unusual, rare, epic, legendary, mythic, ultra, and super. The higher the rarity of the petal, the stronger it is. Some petals gain new properties at a certain rarity. Most petals have health and petal damage like flowers, but also a petal reload. Petals can also be absorbed for experience, which then translates into levels.

5 petals of the same rarity can be crafted for a chance to create 1 petal of the next rarity. If the crafting fails, 1-4 petals are destroyed. The higher the rarity, the smaller the chance of success. This is arguably the most demoralizing aspect of the game.

Petals can do a wide variety of things, which include but are not limited to:

  • Deal damage to mobs
  • Heal the flower
  • Summon friendly mobs to defend the flower
  • Increase flower speed
  • Increase petal rotation speed
  • Increase petal range
  • Push back mobs
  • Revive dead flowers
  • Absolutely nothing
